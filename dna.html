Know-how

The Oath DNA

A house is known by its signatures. Four devices carry the spirit of Oath across everything we make.

The Circle

The O — gathering, return, the unbroken round of a day. Our mark and our aperture, the way the house frames the world.

The Two Dots

Breath and pause. Connection between people, between body and spirit. The smallest device, repeated like a heartbeat.

Laotian Earth Red

Our single point of colour — the vitality of the soil. Used sparingly, like a seal pressed into clay.

Honest Material

Clay, paper, linen, wood, stone. Nothing pretends to be other than it is. Rooted in the real.
