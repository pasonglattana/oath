Privacy

Last updated · June 2026

Oath House respects the people who walk through our doors — online and in person. This page explains, plainly, what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Details you give us when you reserve or write to us — your name, email, phone and any notes.
  • If you subscribe, your email address, so we can send the occasional letter.
  • Basic, anonymous usage data (which pages are visited) to keep the house in good order.

How we use it

To confirm and prepare for your visit, to answer you, and — only if you ask — to send you news of gatherings, records and classes. We do not sell your information. Ever.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from any letter at any time, and you may ask us to show, correct or delete the information we hold about you by writing to reservation@oathhouse.co.

Cookies

We use only what is needed for the site to work and to understand, anonymously, how it is used. No advertising trackers.
