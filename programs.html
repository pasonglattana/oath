Our Program

A practice for every body — breath, movement and stillness, chosen to meet you where you are. Below are the classes held at the studio.

01

Yoga Ball

Balance & Flexibility

Yoga Ball helps with balance and flexibility. The classes suit those with no prior exercise experience or who lack strength, as well as people who sit in an office for long periods.

02

Yoga Calm

Release & Restore

Yoga Calm focuses on addressing most physical problems — muscle tension, office syndrome, and inflexible muscles caused by excessive forms of other exercise.

03

Chair Yoga

Gentle & Supported

Chair Yoga improves balance, especially for those with weak limbs, poor balance or limited movement. It is also suitable for the elderly and can aid physical recovery, particularly for pre-existing injuries or general physical weakness.

04

Yoga Sticks

Alignment & Posture

Yoga Sticks helps with body alignment, stretching, and correcting the body's posture.

05

Swing Yoga

Pilates in Motion

Swing Yoga is a combination of Pilates and body-alignment techniques woven together with stretching.

06

Pilates Mat

Core & Spine

Pilates Mat focuses on the central parts of the body along the spine — the nervous system, the smaller muscle groups and the deep core muscles.

07

Yoga Fly & Yoga Hammock

Five Arts, One Flow

Yoga Fly and Yoga Hammock bring together five types of training — yoga, pilates, ballet, stretching and dancing — into a single aerial routine.

Not sure where to begin? Our instructors will guide you to the right practice.
