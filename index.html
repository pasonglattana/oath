Our Story

Born from
the quiet ground

Oath is not a place you arrive at — it is a place you return to. Founded in the belief that beauty lives in honest things, we exist at the convergence of culture, craft, and the slow intelligence of the natural world.

We are ceremony and gathering. We are the smell of wet earth after rain, the warmth of fired clay, the amber hour before dark. Every gathering here is a small vow — to presence, to authenticity, to the people who have shaped this land before us.

A vow to what is real.
The Space

Our Venue

The Hall

The Stone Hall

A vaulted gathering space for up to 180 guests. Raw limestone walls, warm candlelight, and the hush of thick stone between you and the world.

The Garden

The Burning Garden

An open-air terrace framed by ancient olive trees and fire pits. For dusk rituals, open tables, and the kind of conversation that only starts after dark.

The Studio

The Clay Studio

An intimate workshop and event space for up to 30. Kiln warmth, earth underfoot, and the meditative rhythm of making things by hand.
